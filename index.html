Our Story

Established in 1955 by Sadhuram Jotumal Motwani, M/S Sadhuram Jotumal & Sons has always been committed to manufacturing pure and superior spices. Quality, consistency, and a unique flavor—this is our identity. We provide you with a vast portfolio of natural and organic products, which bring the authentic taste of India to your food.

Established in 2018, S.J. Proteins is a company founded by Ramdas Sadhuram Motwani and Rameshlal Sadhuram Motwani. It is dedicated to manufacturing superior-quality Chana Dal. With an expanding portfolio, we are committed to bringing more products in the future. Our main focus is towards quality and organic products. We offer our dal with both sortex and non-sortex separation for the best quality.

“Quality is achieved when you yourself can use your product without hesitation.”
