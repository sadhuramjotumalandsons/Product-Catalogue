Our Story

Late Shri Sadhuram Jotumal Motwani

Established in 1955 by Late Shri Sadhuram Jotumal Motwani in Akola, Maharashtra, our journey began with gram flour and turmeric. From the very beginning, we have been committed to delivering superior quality and authentic organic products that reflect the true taste of India. Over the years, we have steadily expanded our portfolio, introducing a wide range of powdered spices and whole spices crafted with precision and care. Our manufacturing process combines tradition with modern standards to ensure consistency, purity, and excellence. With a strong focus on quality and trust, we continue to grow as a recognized name in the Indian spice industry. Moving forward, we remain dedicated to innovation and are committed to bringing new and diverse products to our valued customers in the future.

Late Shri Ramdas Sadhuram Motwani

Shri Rameshlal Sadhuram Motwani

Established in 2018 by brothers Shri Ramdas Sadhuram Motwani and Shri Rameshlal Sadhuram Motwani, SJ Proteins has emerged as a trusted name in the field of pulses. With a strong foundation in manufacturing high-quality chana dal, we are committed to delivering products that reflect purity, nutrition, and authenticity. Our focus on superior quality and organic standards ensures that every grain meets the expectations of health-conscious consumers. We offer both sortex and non-sortex chana dal, catering to diverse customer needs with consistency and reliability. Looking ahead, SJ Proteins continues to expand its horizons, working towards introducing more products to enrich everyday kitchens. Our promise remains rooted in excellence, integrity, and customer trust.
